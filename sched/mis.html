ABOUT

This course is designed to provide students with a basic understanding of how Information Systems are used in organizations for meeting strategic andoperational goals. To that end, students will acquire skills using current end-user software for communication, data transformation, collaboration, and problemsolving. The course also covers software and hardware components, information structures, basic business organization and processes, information system security, and networks
